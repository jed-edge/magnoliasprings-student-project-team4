Open Mic Performers

Got a talent to share? The open mic runs Saturday from 10:30 AM to 12:30 PM on the community stage at the courthouse lawn.

  • Sign up at the Main Information Booth (corner of Spring Street and Broad Street) starting at 9:30 AM. Slots are first come, first served.
  • Each performer gets a 5-minute slot. Singers, musicians, poets, and family acts welcome.
  • All open mic performances must be family-friendly.
  • A basic PA system, microphone, and one guitar amp are provided.

Good to Know

  • General admission is free! Only the Saturday Night Heritage Concert ($15) and the Taste of the Springs Food Tent ($10) require tickets.
  • Public parking is available at the Magnolia Springs High School lot and the Community Center. ADA-accessible parking is behind City Hall with free golf cart shuttles to the entrance.
  • Pets are not permitted on the courthouse lawn or in the food courts. Leashed service animals are always welcome.
  • Schedule is subject to change. For more details, check the Questions page or sign up for email updates above!
