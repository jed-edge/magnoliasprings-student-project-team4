2024 Honorary Marshal: Eli 'Rocket' Thomas

To everyone who meets him, eight-year-old Eli Thomas is a burst of joyful energy, a boy with a quick laugh, a deep love for building elaborate treehouses, and an infectious, wide smile that can brighten the gloomiest Georgia afternoon.

Eli was diagnosed with acute lymphoblastic leukemia just before his sixth birthday, two years ago. This turned his world upside down. What followed was a difficult, grueling journey of hospital stays, intense treatments, and months of feeling unlike his old, active self. Throughout this "uphill battle," Eli showed a level of courage and quiet strength that left his family and doctors in awe.

Now, Eli is in remission, and his smile is bigger and brighter than ever. His love for life, once a small ember, has blazed back into a roaring fire. He is a local kid who knows the value of community, and he is a powerful reminder that even in the face of daunting challenges, hope and resilience can bloom.

Vendor Spotlight

This season, the Autumn Jubilee is proud to feature Sarah Jenkins, the talented hands and heart behind Juniper’s Clay and Craft..

From the rough bark of a Magnolia tree to the rich colors of fall foliage, she uses local, natural clay bodies and hand-mixed glazes to create pottery that feels both timeless and personal. You can visit Sarah and see her live pottery demonstrations at Booth #34 in the Main Marketplace.
